About Us

Compassionate Healthcare

My mission is to provide you with personalized, high-quality care. I am dedicated to improving and maintaining your health through the selection of the optimum preventative care, advanced diagnostics and treatments.

Experienced Cardiologist Care

I offer consultant cardiologist expertise to investigate heart related symptoms and disease and optimise your health and symptoms. I am a UK trained specialist centre consultant cardiologist with expertise across general adult cardiology and aditional expertise inadvanced, non-invasive cardiac imaging.

A Personal Approach

My goal is to improve and maintain your overall health and to empower you with an understanding of your condition and wellness plan.

How I Help

Preventative Care

We offer a variety of preventative care services such as annual physical exams, vardiovascular fitness assessment and caardiovascular risk assessment.

Symptoms

We offer assessment of all potential cardiac symptoms including chest pain, breathlessness, syncope (blackouts), palpitations/arrhythmia and lethargy.

Diagnostics and Treatment

You can rely on our accredited consultant cardiologist doctor expertise to treat all of your heart health needs such as angina/coronary heart disease, hypertension, heart failure, elevated cholesterol, angina, arrhythmia, valvular heart disease, cardiomyopathy, inherited heart disease and syncope. We alsooffer second opinions and medical reports.
